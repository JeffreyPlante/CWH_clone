Our Philosophy

Together with our community, we are on the pursuit of the following principles:

Leadership

Develop creativity, courage, and confidence in all participants to be the leaders of tomorrow.

Skill-Building

Teach students the fundamental skills to succeed in future coding courses and pathways.

Impact

Increase the number of young women interested in pursuing a career in technology.

Community

Create a supportive environment of women in technology, where we are empowered to speak our minds, celebrate our successes, and support each other through our journey.

Inclusiveness

Promote equity, diversity, and equality in technology opportunities and education.

Possibility

Expand participants perspective of the endless opportunities in the technology field.

With the emergence of technology as a basic necessity of our times, the number of jobs to support technology is growing astronomically.

Our mission is to provide access and opportunity for everyone.
